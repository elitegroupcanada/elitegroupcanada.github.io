ABOUT US

Elite Group is an electromechanical contracting company based in Ontario. Since our establishment in 2018, we have built a reputation for delivering exceptional services in the field of HVAC and plumbing. Our dedicated team specializes in providing comprehensive solutions for commercial properties, ensuring that every project is executed to perfection.


Elite Group serves cities in the GTA and across Ontario. From small-scale to large-scale upgrades, we have the expertise and resources to handle projects of any size and complexity.


At Elite Group, we believe in excellence through efficiency. Our experienced professionals work closely with clients, understanding their specific requirements and efficiently delivering tailored solutions that meet their needs. With their experience, we seamlessly manage every aspect of the project, from procurement to project management.


Elite Group is the ultimate investment for reliable and expert electromechanical contracting services to bring your vision to life with exceptional quality and precision.
